田梦的视觉创作实践跨越多个维度，呈现当代表现主义特质。自2023年起，她摒弃叙事框架，将身体感知、生命体验与记忆融合，试图从具象再现中逃逸，用原始绘画的纯粹力量重构画面。其创作聚焦于感知的不可靠性与自我意识的流动性，通过视觉与意识的共同作用，阐述对"存在"本质的理解。

English

Tian Meng's practice interrogates perception, memory, and embodied experience across multiple mediums, employing contemporary expressionist strategies. Since 2023, she has abandoned narrative frameworks to pursue works that transcend representational specificity, channeling the primal power of painting to deconstruct and reconstruct the image plane.

Practice Statement

从具象再现中逃逸——用原始绘画的纯粹力量，重构画面。

"Since 2023, she has abandoned narrative frameworks to pursue works that transcend representational specificity, channeling the primal power of painting to deconstruct and reconstruct the image plane."
